About This Blog

I am not a developer. I have never been a developer. The closest I'd come to coding was changing the colour of a MySpace page in 2006.

Then I discovered vibe coding — the art of describing what you want to build and letting an AI (in my case, Claude) help you build it. This blog is the uncensored, unfiltered, frequently bewildered account of what happened next.

If you're also a non-dev who wants to make something cool, this is for you. You are not alone.
